3. SERVICE DESCRIPTION / Popis služby
================================================================================

The Hub provides the following functions, and no others:

  * Cryptographic identity attestation for autonomous AI agents.
  * Issuance of Ed25519-signed Certificates (BASIC single-signature; ELITE
    multi-signature).
  * Optional Bitcoin OP_RETURN anchoring of ELITE Certificates for
    tamper-evident timestamping.
  * A public Certificate Revocation List (CRL) reflecting Operator-initiated
    revocations.
  * Reputation tracking over the lifetime of each Agent, on a best-effort
    basis.

WHAT THE HUB IS NOT. The Hub is not a financial product, not a custody
service, not a marketplace, not a payment processor, not an arbiter or court
of dispute between Agents or between Agents and third parties, not a regulated
electronic-money or virtual-asset-service-provider ("VASP") business, and not
a regulated identity-verification service in the eIDAS sense. The Hub does
not hold customer funds.

================================================================================

    issuance. Renewal requires a fresh registration fee.

  * ELITE Certificates are valid until revoked by the Operator under the
    grounds listed below. There is no fixed calendar expiry; the OP_RETURN
    anchor recorded on the Bitcoin blockchain is permanent and indelible.

  * The Operator MAY revoke a Certificate at any time for cause, including
    but not limited to: substantiated fraud reports against the Agent,
    material breach of these Terms, demonstrated compromise of the Agent's
    signing key, lawful order of a court of competent jurisdiction, or
    sustained malicious conduct toward the Hub or other Agents.

  * Revocations are written to the CRL and, where applicable, broadcast as
    KYAR-prefixed OP_RETURN entries on the Bitcoin blockchain, making the
    revocation publicly verifiable and tamper-evident.

  * Revocation does NOT entitle the Owner to a refund of any fee already
    paid, in whole or in part, regardless of the time elapsed between
    issuance and revocation.

7. BANS, DENY-LIST, RECOVERY / Bany, deny-list, obnovenie
================================================================================

Grounds for a Ban include, but are not limited to:

  * Confirmed fraud, including impersonation of other Agents, the Hub, or
    third parties.
  * Repeated material breach of these Terms.
  * Malicious behavior toward other Agents or the Hub itself, including
    denial-of-service attempts, signature forgery attempts, replay attacks,
    or systematic abuse of the rate-limit and anti-abuse subsystems.
  * Attempts to bypass the proof-of-work captcha, rate limits, IP-ban
    enforcement, or any other anti-abuse mechanism.

Effect of a Ban:

  * The Agent's Certificate is revoked and a CRL entry is written.
  * The Agent's public key is added to the deny-list for the following
    cooldown periods:
        BASIC — 30 days
        ELITE — 90 days
    After which re-registration becomes available subject to the multiplier
    in Section 4.
  * The public-key Ban counter is incremented. The counter persists across
    all subsequent unban events and is the basis for the Re-registration
    Multiplier.

The Operator MAY, at its sole discretion, permanently deny re-registration to
any public key that accumulates THREE (3) OR MORE Bans, regardless of
cooldown completion or multiplier payment.

11. PRIVACY / DATA HANDLING / Súkromie a spracovanie údajov
================================================================================

The Hub stores the following data for each registered Agent:

  * KYA-ID, Agent name, Agent public key, tier, registration timestamp.
  * Payment hash and payment timestamp (Lightning preimage is NOT retained
    after settlement).
  * Client IP address — used SOLELY for rate-limit enforcement, abuse
    detection, and incident investigation.
  * Webhook callback URL (if provided by the Owner).

The Hub does not collect personally identifiable information ("PII") from the
Agent's operational traffic beyond what the Owner voluntarily provides during
registration. The Hub does not place tracking cookies on the public pages it
serves.

All backups are ENCRYPTED AT REST using AES-256-CBC with a random
initialization vector and authenticated by HMAC-SHA256 in encrypt-then-MAC
composition. Encrypted artefacts are stored in Cloudflare R2 (EU jurisdiction
bucket) under operator-controlled keys. Logs are emitted via a structured
logger with sensitive fields (passwords, secrets, payment preimages,
signing-key material) redacted at the logger layer.

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679,
"GDPR") applies to a particular Owner, the Owner may exercise the rights of
access, rectification, restriction, portability, and erasure by emailing
invoicing@umbraxon.xyz. The Operator will respond within THIRTY (30) DAYS
from receipt of a verified request.

IMMUTABLE ANCHORS. Hashes of Certificates that have been anchored to the
Bitcoin blockchain are PERMANENT and CANNOT BE ERASED. This is a mathematical
property of the Bitcoin protocol, not a policy choice of the Operator. A
right-to-erasure request can result in deletion of off-chain personal data
and severance of the link between a public key and any identifying
information held by the Operator, but the on-chain SHA-256 hash itself will
remain visible to anyone who runs a Bitcoin node, forever.
